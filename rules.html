Instructions
  1. Enter player 1 or player 2 names.
  2. Select your choice for either player 1 or player 2.
  3. Upon selection the timer begins.
  4. You can change your choice until the timer hits 0.
  5. If player 2 has not chosen, the computer will make it for them.
  6. Once the timer hits 0, the winner will be chosen!
